VerifAI: Fake News Detector

Web-based application designed to detect and analyze fake news using advanced NLP techniques; The project integrates the BERT model with transfer learning to assess the authenticity of news content; Enhanced BERT's efficiency by fine-tuning it on specific datasets related to news and misinformation; Employed batch processing, froze BERT layers for transfer learning, and developed a custom neural network 'BERT_Arch' for binary classification; The softmax function is applied to the output layer of the BERT-based neural network model to display the likelihood of each sentence being "fake" as a percentage; Developed a user-friendly interface using Flask and HTML, enhancing accessibility and ease of use
